## Description

PostCommand is the complete workforce management platform for licensed security companies. Built for security professionals by security professionals.

**FEATURES FOR OFFICERS:**
• GPS-verified clock in/out at your assigned sites
• Submit incident reports with AI-assisted narrative writing
• View your schedule and request shift swaps
• Complete training courses and earn certificates
• Request PTO and view approval status
• Receive real-time push notifications for schedule changes
• SOS emergency alert with instant supervisor notification
• QR code patrol checkpoint scanning with route verification
• Equipment check-out and check-in tracking

**FEATURES FOR SUPERVISORS & ADMINISTRATORS:**
• Real-time officer location map across all sites
• Schedule creation, management, and auto-assignment
• Timesheet review, approval, and payroll export (ADP/Paychex)
• Pay period close flow with no-show tracking
• Incident report workflow with multi-field form and approval pipeline
• Training assignment and completion tracking with certificates
• AI training suggestions based on completion data
• HR document management, e-signatures, and policy management
• Equipment & uniform inventory with QR codes
• Client portal for site-specific activity
• Geofence violation monitoring and alerts
• Full audit log for all system activity

**COMPLIANCE TOOLS:**
• Multi-state licensing management (MD, DC, VA, PA)
• CCW reciprocity reference map (all 50 states + DC)
• Credential expiration monitoring and automated reminders
• GPS patrol checkpoint verification with route playback
• Performance review tracking
• Employee credential management (guard cards, firearms certs, etc.)

**BILLING:**
• Standard: $2.50/user/month — single location
• Professional: $5.00/user/month — multi-location + AI features
• Subscriptions managed at postcommand.app (not in-app)

PostCommand is used exclusively by authorized security company employees. Account creation requires employer invitation — there is no public sign-up.

## Review Notes (for Apple Reviewer)

PostCommand is a private enterprise workforce management tool for licensed security companies. Please note:

1. **Login required** — All users must be invited by their employer. There is no public sign-up.

   **Demo account:** reviewer@postcommand.app / PostCommand2026!
   **Test company:** NPS (pre-loaded with employees, sites, and schedules)

2. **Location permission** — The app requests "When In Use" location access to verify employees are physically present at their assigned work sites when clocking in or out. Location is only captured during clock-in/out events and patrol checkpoints — not tracked continuously.

3. **Camera permission** — Used for clock-in photo verification (confirming officer identity) and documenting incidents at work sites. This is a standard security industry compliance requirement.

4. **Push notifications** — Used for schedule updates, timesheet approvals, SOS emergency alerts, and training reminders.

5. **No in-app purchases** — Subscriptions are managed exclusively at postcommand.app. This complies with Apple's guidelines for B2B enterprise apps.

6. **GPS features** — Require a physical device. Xcode Simulator does not support GPS geofencing accurately.

7. **AI features** — Require active internet connection. The AI narrative polish and training suggestions call backend edge functions.

8. **Content** — All content is employer-controlled. The app contains no user-generated public content — all data is private to the employing security company.
